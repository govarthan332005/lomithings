How We Build Your Website

Our streamlined process ensures we deliver a high-quality website that perfectly represents you.

1. Information Gathering

We start by collecting all necessary information about your background, skills, projects, and preferences to create a website that truly represents you.

2. Design & Approval

Our designers create a custom mockup based on your requirements. We'll share it with you for feedback and make any necessary adjustments until you're completely satisfied.

3. Development

Once the design is approved, our development team brings it to life with clean, efficient code and implements all the requested features and functionality.

4. Testing & Optimization

We thoroughly test your website across different devices and browsers to ensure everything works perfectly. We also optimize it for speed and performance.

5. Launch & Support

After final approval, we launch your website and provide you with all necessary files and instructions. We offer 7 days of free support to address any issues or questions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

How long does it take to build a portfolio website?

Typically, we deliver portfolio websites within 0-1 business days from the date we receive all the required information. Business websites may take 7-14 days depending on complexity.

Do I need to purchase a domain and hosting separately?

Yes, you'll need to purchase domain and hosting separately. We can recommend affordable options and help you set everything up for a small additional fee, or you can handle it yourself.

What information do I need to provide?

You'll need to provide your bio/about information, project details, skills, contact information, social media links, and any images or content you want included on your site.

Do you offer website maintenance services?

Yes, we offer website maintenance packages starting at ₹299/month. This includes updates, security patches, and minor content changes. You can also request one-time updates for a small fee.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept payments via UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer, and major credit/debit cards. We require full payment before starting work on your project.
